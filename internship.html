Internship / Inplant Training Rules

Maintain professionalism and discipline to successfully complete the program.

  1. Minimum 80% attendance is mandatory for receiving the certificate.
  2. Punctuality and discipline must be strictly maintained during all sessions.
  3. Interns must follow mentor instructions, training schedules and timelines.
  4. Daily tasks and assignments must be completed and submitted on time.
  5. No plagiarism or copying of project work from others or online sources.
  6. Mobile and internet usage must be limited to learning and project purposes only.
  7. Confidential project data, code and materials must not be shared externally.
  8. Professional behavior and respectful communication are expected at all times.
  9. Certificate issuance is subject to mentor evaluation and successful task completion.
  10. No refund or certificate will be provided in case of early exit or rule violations.
